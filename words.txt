Ethiopia: Landlocked country in the Horn of Africa with 112 million people.
"Cradle of Humanity": Home to oldest human remains ever discovered.
Diverse landscapes: Great Rift Valley, Simien Mountains, and more.
Rich culture: 80+ ethnic groups with unique traditions and languages.
Renowned cuisine: Injera and wat are popular flavorful dishes.
Historical significance: Resistance against colonization, inspiring other nations' independence.
Economic growth: Focus on agriculture, manufacturing, and construction sectors.
Coffee powerhouse: Leading exporter in the global coffee industry.
Challenges faced: Poverty, instability, and ethnic tensions persistently present.
Architectural marvels: Ancient rock-hewn churches of Lalibela, attracting global visitors.
